This is also a personal recipe of mine.

This is a very simple recipe beacause its only the seasoning for the beef. Honestly with how good it comes out it would be hard to mess it up The flavor of the beef is very versatile.

As stated before this is only a recipe for the seasoning. After making your taco meat like this you will never buy seasoning packets again I personally garuntee it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 table spoon Chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 to 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (depending on how spicy you like)
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Step by Step

  1. mix seasonings into ground beef
  2. add the beef and seasoning mixture into a oiled pan
  3. cook on medium heat until brown
  4. enjoy on your tacos, nachos, burritos, or where ever you see fit!
