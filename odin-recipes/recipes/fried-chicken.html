The Best Fried Chicken

I put my word on it this is the best fried chicken recipe. Its my recipe by the way.

Chances are you already have most if not all the ingredients for this recipe in your kitchen. This is a very simple yet delicious recipe. This recipe is good for fried chicken nuggets, wings, sandwiches, tenders, ect. I am not sure how well it would work in an air fryer because I dont own one.

I know this is just a practice website but in all seriousness I highly reccomend trying this recipe out. You will not be dissapointed. This rescipe is desgined to work for two chicken breasts but I would highly reccomend doubling it for two sets of chicken breast because youll wish you had more.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breast cut thin
  • Frying oil
  • 1 1/4 cups Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Powdered Sugar (not to be confused with regular sugar)
  • Half a cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • Half a cup Dill Pickle juice
  • 1 teaspoon Chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Step by Step

  1. Cut chicken to desired sizes (I recomend cutting it thin for more crispys)
  2. put chicken into bowl with half cup milk and half cup dill pickle juice and let that sit for atleast 20 minutes
  3. add 1 egg into the marinade and wisk it in
  4. heat up oil for fying on medium heat
  5. mix in a seperate bowl flour, powdered sugar, paprika, chili powder, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.(I reccomend using a bowl with a lid so yu can shake it)
  6. add chicken from marinade into the batter in small batches
  7. add chicken from batter to the frying oil in maintainable batches
  8. Cook until golden brown
  9. Eat and enjoy!
